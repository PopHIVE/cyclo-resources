This report provides an overview of the occurrence of selected reported infectious diseases in Ohio, including geographic distribution, demographics, and dates of illness. Non-perinatal hepatitis B, hepatitis C, HIV/AIDS, sexually transmitted diseases, and tuberculosis surveillance data are not included. Data for these diseases are available on the Ohio Department of Health Data and Statistics website. To protect individual patient privacy, some data may be unavailable if fewer than ten cases are reported over the chosen timeframe.
